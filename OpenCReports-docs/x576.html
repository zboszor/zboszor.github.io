Report section

This section may occur in a wider context, with either <OpenCReport>, or <Part> being the toplevel node, or standalone. Examples: 

<OpenCReport>
    <Part>
        <pr>
            <pd>
                <Report>
                    ...
                </Report>
            </pd>
        </pr>
    </Part>
</OpenCReport>

<Part>
	<pr>
		<pd>
			<Report>
				...
			</Report>
		</pd>
	</pr>
</Part>

<Report>
	...
</Report>

When <Report>> is the toplevel node, parent nodes for <Part>, <pr> and <pd> are implicitly created. Subsections and many attributes specific to these parent nodes can be used as shortcuts in the <Report>> node.
